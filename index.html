Inspect any phone number.

Validity, country, line type, every format, local time, QR & call links — computed locally with Google's libphonenumber. Most checkers send your number to a server; this one never does.

How it works

1

Type or paste a number (with a + country code, or pick a default region).

2

DialCheck parses it locally — validity, country, type, formats, time zone, links.

3

Copy a format, grab the QR/vCard, or bulk-clean a whole list and export CSV.

Note: carrier and a person's identity can't be derived offline (and DialCheck never tries to). Number portability means the prefix can't reliably name the current carrier; accurate carrier needs a paid HLR lookup.
